About

NewsBlur is web application which provides you news of all around the world in a summarised way. NewsBlur selects latest and best news from multiple national and international sources and summarises them to present in short and crisp 60 words or less format,personalized for you. All summarised stories contain only headlines and facts, no opinions, to help you stay informed with the current affairs. Whether it’s the latest government policies or shakeups in bollywood, get them covered and delivered super fast! Get updated with the latest news and current affairs in a jiffy!

News Readers - Read Summaries of All Breaking News

My Feed- A feed of latest stories, videos and other content personalised only for you!
